Goa Trip Plan by pH

Beaches • Scooter Ride • Food • Forts

Fountainhas Lane

Colorful Portuguese houses and street art. Perfect for a morning photo walk.

Cabo de Rama Fort

Cliff-side fort with ocean views. Ideal for sunset scenes and drone shots.

Cola Beach

Hidden beach with a lagoon and quiet vibes. Try kayaking or chilling here.

Butterfly Beach

Remote beach accessible by boat. Clean, quiet, and sometimes dolphins too.

Margao City

Goan markets, snacks, local culture. A great spot for food and shopping.

Parra Road

Iconic coconut tree road from Bollywood movies. Best experienced on a scooter.

Things to Do

  • Ride scooters along beach roads
  • Sunset at Cabo de Rama
  • Kayak at Cola Beach
  • Beach games with friends
  • Try Goan seafood & shop in Margao

Budget Hotel Suggestion

Hotel: Silver Sands Sunshine, Candolim

2-star hotel with pool and beach nearby. Budget friendly for groups.
