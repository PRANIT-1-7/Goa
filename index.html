Goa Trip Plan with Friends

Explore Roads, Beaches, and Adventures

Fountainhas Lane

Explore Portuguese-style colorful houses and charming narrow lanes perfect for photoshoots.

Cabo de Rama Fort

Stunning cliffside fort with a view of the Arabian Sea. A peaceful place with epic drone shots.

Cola Beach

Hidden paradise with a freshwater lagoon and beach – perfect for chill and kayak fun.

Butterfly Beach

Remote beach surrounded by dense forest. Ideal for boating and watching dolphins.

Margao City

Hub of local shopping and food. Great place to try Goan street food with friends.

Parra Road

Iconic coconut tree-lined road featured in movies like 'Dear Zindagi' – a must-scooter ride route.

Activities to Do

  • Beach volleyball & frisbee
  • Sunset watching
  • Kayaking at Cola Beach
  • Scooter ride from Parra to Cabo
  • Try Goan seafood and local dishes

Recommended Hotel (3-Star, Budget Friendly)

Hotel: Silver Sands Sunshine, Candolim

Pool, close to beach, great for groups
